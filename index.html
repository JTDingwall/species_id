    About This App

    Bamfield Species ID is designed to help undergraduate students learn to identify common intertidal marine animals found in Bamfield, British Columbia.

    Species: 25 common intertidal species

    Games: Flashcards, Quiz, Matching, Timed Challenge

    Offline: Load once, then works without internet

    Images sourced from Wikimedia Commons under CC-BY-SA and public domain licenses. Species data curated for Bamfield intertidal zone.
