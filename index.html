    🌊 About This App

    Bamfield Species ID is designed to help undergraduate students learn to identify common intertidal and nearshore marine species found in Bamfield, British Columbia.

    Species: 125 intertidal, subtidal, & nearshore fish species

    Photos: Sourced from Wikimedia Commons (CC-BY-SA / public domain)

    Games: Flashcards, Quiz, Matching, Timed Challenge

    Offline capable: PWA with full offline service worker caching
